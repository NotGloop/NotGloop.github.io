Firestarter Rules

By playing on Firestarter's servers, you agree to follow these rules. Failure to comply will result in a suspension from both the Firestarter Minecraft Realms and the Firestarter Discord server.

  1. Respect everyone and be ethical. Don't bully others and/or use racial slurs. Cursing is allowed, as long as it is not directed towards any particular person.
  2. Don't spam the chat (fancy text and repeated phrases count as spam). Command spam is also strictly disallowed.
  3. Don't grief, whether the land is claimed or not. Taking items from unclaimed chests (referred to as "raiding"), is allowed. Lava casts count as griefing.
  4. Don't advertise other servers or websites. Posting malicious links is a bannable offense.
  5. Don't make traps (including TP traps). Bypassing the PvP toggle system is not allowed.
  6. Don't use modified/hacked clients. Cheating of any form is highly bannable. Mods such as OptiFine and certain minimaps (JourneyMap, Xaero's, and Voxelmap) are allowed.
  7. Don't use alternate accounts on the server. On top of that, we prohibit use of other accounts to claim extra event rewards. You must also now share your account with others.
  8. Claims must be no closer than 100 blocks apart, unless they are your own claims.
  9. Don't build lag machines or anything that majorly degrades server performance. Staff may ask you to remove large builds that are causing performance issues.
  10. Whatever the staff says is what you follow.
  11. And most importantly, use common sense.

Failure to comply will result in a suspension, the length of which is chosen by our staff on a case-by-case basis. Most offenses, such as griefing, have defined ban lengths on first offense, with future offenses having longer lengths.
